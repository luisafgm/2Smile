Veneers are aesthetics restorations made on the visual side of your teeth, mainly the front ones, this will give you a natural and attractive smile. Veneers are also used to enhance and cover different issues, natural abrasion, bruxism wear, correct gaps, improve color. This are also used to correct asymmetries or unusual teeth shape, length, and misalignment of natural teeth.


We always use top of the market materials, and you can choose from the following recommended options. High aesthetics resin veneers and ceramic veneers are the options available. Below you will find characteristics, advantages, and disadvantages of these.


It consists of a simple and direct procedure in which specialists will apply a high esthetic resin restorative material, which gives you the possibility of improving the proportion, size, shape, and color of your teeth, in the front area of the tooth which can be performed with or without minimal abrasive wear of the tooth enamel according to the conditions of your natural teeth. This is an aesthetic, minimally invasive, painless, and quick solution, it is an excellent option for those patients who want to improve the appearance of their mouth and smile.


Are custom made and elaborated from high resistant porcelain and glass. Fabricated using latest technology, in a facility close from our clinic. These ceramic veneers will last longer and have great adhesivity, the fit and aesthetics are amazing.


Advantages:

  • You can choose color, size, and style.
  • You will obtain top of the line technology, allowing to have the greatest aesthetics looks compared with other treatments.
  • It can be done in four or five appointments.
  • These restorations are considered minimally invasive. Generally, is not necessary to wear down your teeth for fits or adjustments.
  • Compared with resin veneers, ceramic veneers have a great aesthetic property these do not lose shine, polish, and color.
  • Highly resistant to fractures
  • Higher survival and success rate over time.
  • They are not easily pigmented with tobacco use.
  • They do not lose their polish, shine and color.
    Disadvantages:
  • If necessary, these are more difficult to repair.
  • It is recommended to change the veneers in a maximum of 20 years depending on their condition and care.
  • The cost is higher than resin veneers, but the benefit to cost relation it is a better investment for yourself.
