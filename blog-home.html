San Cipriano & River Adventure Day Tour

If you want to add some natural adventure to your trip to Cali, you are reading the right page. Take this full day tour to San Cipriano and live an experience like no other else. Get to know “la brujita” a rare transportation mean powered by a motorcycle and the only way to access to San Cipriano. Swim in its river, try traditional food at a local restaurant and enjoy this natural reserve located in Buenaventura, two hours away from Cali.
